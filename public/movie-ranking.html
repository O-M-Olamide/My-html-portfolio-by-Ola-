The Best Movies According to Olamide

My top 3 movies of all time

Bigil (2019)

Bigil is an Indian sports drama starring Vijay in a dual role as Michael, a local gangster, and Bigil, a former football player who must step in as a coach to lead a women’s football team to success. The film combines action and drama, touching on themes of empowerment.

Bad Boys for Life (2020)

The third installment in the Bad Boys franchise, this action-comedy reunites detectives Mike Lowrey (Will Smith) and Marcus Burnett (Martin Lawrence) as they face off against a powerful drug cartel in Miami. The duo, dealing with age and their changing dynamics, must come together for one last ride to take down a deadly enemy from Mike’s past.

It Ends With Us

It Ends With Us is currently being adapted into a film based on Colleen Hoover’s bestselling novel, but it hasn’t been released as of now. The story centers on a woman named Lily who falls for a charming neurosurgeon named Ryle, only to face unexpected challenges as past traumas resurface, threatening their relationship. The novel explores heavy themes like love, resilience, and difficult choices in relationships.
